Cars are the number one source of pollution globally.

Our mission at Tuscaloosa Environmental Association is to change that.

That's why we've created a platform that allows users to easily compare electric cars to traditional gas-powered vehicles. Ultimately, our goal is to help users make an informed decision when it comes to buying an electric car. We believe that by providing the information needed to compare electric and gas-powered cars, we can help accelerate the transition to a more sustainable future. Our team of experts are constantly monitoring the latest trends and developments in the electric vehicle market, and we strive to provide the most comprehensive and reliable data possible.
